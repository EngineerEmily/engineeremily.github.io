Made @ The Tech

Spring 2015


Welcome to Made @ The Tech!

This spring The Tech produced a new magazine to share our favorite things – new exhibits, fun challenges, inspiring stories – with our favorite people: You.

The highlight of this piece is our 2014 Annual Report, which we present along with our unending gratitude for the donors, volunteers, educators and members who bring this building to life. There are also features on new IMAX releases, our signature programs The Tech Awards and The Tech Challenge, improvements and additions to our educational offerings, and much more.

Please enjoy, and thank you for being part of The Tech community!
