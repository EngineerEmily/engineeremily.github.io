About Cory

Cory Wolbach is a councilmember for the City of Palo Alto. He is optimistic about the future of his hometown. Palo Alto's greatest asset is the ingenuity of its residents. As a proud graduate of Gunn High School and University of California, San Diego, and as a longtime resident of the Palo Verde neighborhood, he cherishes what Palo Alto has provided his family: home, diverse community, open space and parks, good jobs, amazing schools. He looks forward to working to ensure all those opportunities for the next generation of Palo Altans.
